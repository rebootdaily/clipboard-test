Clipboard v6.4.1 - Point Tools Restored

Restored to Point-to-Point mode:
- Interior Freeform Line: two taps, no dimension required, does not affect area.
- Garage Cut-Out: tap opposite corners; area is subtracted from floor/GLA.
- Open to Below: tap opposite corners; area is subtracted from floor/GLA.
- Exterior Walls remains the default Point-to-Point workflow.

Smart Sketch Beta remains available and unchanged for continued testing.

Cut-out dimensions are inferred from the sketch scale between the two selected corners.
