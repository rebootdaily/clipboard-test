Clipboard v6.4.3 - Precision Cut-Outs + Smart Auto-Close

This build combines the two latest sketch revisions:

1. Precision Garage / Open-to-Below Cut-Outs
- Tap an anchor corner.
- Enter exact length and width.
- Select the quadrant/direction.
- Tap an existing cut-out to revise dimensions, direction, anchor, or delete it.

2. Smart Sketch Auto-Close
- After at least two walls, draw the next wall back toward the green START point.
- When the endpoint enters the 32-screen-pixel snap zone, START turns gold and the preview says RELEASE TO CLOSE.
- Releasing snaps exactly to START, closes the polygon, prevents a tiny gap, and recalculates area.
- The closing wall is marked CALC because its final length is derived from the geometry.

Point-to-Point walls, interior freeform lines, Geometry Assist, voice measurements, pinch zoom, and two-finger pan remain available.
