Clipboard v5.0a - Directional Sketch Engine

Primary changes:
- Tap the sketch grid to set the starting corner.
- Enter each exterior wall length and tap Up, Down, Left, or Right.
- Supports decimal feet and feet/inches entry.
- Automatic wall numbering and polygon area calculation.
- Configurable closure tolerance: 0.25, 0.50, 1.00, or 2.00 feet.
- Prompts to close and balance when the ending point is within tolerance.
- Estimated closure option when outside tolerance, with the discrepancy stored in sketch data.
- Fit-to-sketch zoom.
- Open-to-below subtraction, freeform lines, annotations, floor copies, and separate outbuildings remain available.

Field sequence:
1. Select Structure and Floor.
2. Select Directional Exterior.
3. Tap the starting corner on the grid.
4. Enter a wall length.
5. Tap a direction arrow.
6. Repeat around the exterior.
7. Close the footprint when prompted or use Close Footprint.
